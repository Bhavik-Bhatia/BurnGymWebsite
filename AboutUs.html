About Us?

We are a bunch of people passionate about health
and with us you will fall in love with fitness!

Burn Gym opened its doors in March 2014. We pride ourselves on creating a creative and professional fitness environment, a friendly and knowledgeable staff composed entirely of experts. Our many amenities such as yoga classes, weights and cardio equipment, saunas, and shower facilities for members can make the gym a big part of their lives.

Our Vision

“To enhance the quality of life in the communities, we serve through our fitness philosophy, facilities, programs, and products and to instill in the lives of people everywhere the value of health and fitness.Our one of a kind, rockstar instructors guide riders through an inspirational, meditative fitness experience that’s designed to benefit the body, mind, and soul.”
